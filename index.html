About

Hi, I'm Vinit Shahdeo.
A dedicated Web & Android Developer who is very much fascinated about UI/UX designing, who enjoy designing logos, posters and infographics and who is looking forward to execute few IoT projects.
I am proud alumunus of DPS Bokaro & I have also exprienced life at Kota. Presently, I'm undergrad student at VIT University Vellore.
This is what I am professionally, Let me give a try to share what kind of person exactly I am, here it goes:
I am a person who is positive about every aspect of life. Firstly, I am grateful for the many things I have in my life - an abundant life, a safe place to live in, proper education, supporting parents, good health, few but faithful friends - and I hope to give back to society by doing my job as an IT professional.
I have deep abiding love for learning, building and designing the things. I consistently set short-term goals for myself. Then, once I’ve defined the benchmarks, I take necessary steps to achieve those milestones. Being tireless seeker of knowledge, I always look at where I can improve and strive for perfection in things which I do. I enjoy my ongoing tasks and multitasking. I always keep an eye on my target.

There are many things which I like to do, to see, and to experience. I like to have fun with my buddies, I like to get up late, I like to bunk long boring lectures(if I can’t, I kill time by staring out of the window or checking WhatsApp messages or sleeping with my eyes open) and I like to laugh. I love helping, interacting and meeting the needs of people. I have an especially soft heart for people who have been left behind due to lack of opportunities.
My father is my ideal person in my life. I get inspiration from him, as he is the source of constant support & help in all spheres of my life.
I love music – a good inclination towards Indi Pop & a little of western. I do selectively watch movies mostly Comedy Drama Bollywood Movies. Blogging/Facebooking is my favorite pastime. I am Quora addict too. When I am not doing anything, I play badminton – the only game which I use to play.
Ping me, nothing brings me more joy than being able to connect with new people to expand my worldview and I’m always happy to help.
